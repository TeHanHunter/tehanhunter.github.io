About

I’m a researcher working across multiple facets of exoplanet science. I led the TESS–Gaia Light Curves (TGLC) effort and published papers establishing TGLC as a community resource. I also led the analysis of TOI-5344 b, a rare giant planet around an M dwarf. My toolkit includes the NEIDSpecMatch algorithm for stellar parameter estimation and the SURFSUP survey of ultra-fast rotators.
